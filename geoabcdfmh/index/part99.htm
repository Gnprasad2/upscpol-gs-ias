Meso-regions of the Northern Plains of India


On the basis of geo-climatic and topographical characteristics, the Northern Plains of India may be divided into the following four meso-regions (Fig. 2.19):


1. The Plains of Rajasthan


2. The Punjab Haryana Plains


3. The Ganga Plains


4. The Brahmaputra Plains.
