Luni River


The Luni River rises near Ajmer in the Aravalli Range and receives a few streams like the Bandi, Sukri and the.Jawai only on its left bank. The streams w hich drain the western flank of the Aravalli Range south of Ajmer are seasonal. The Luni turns brackish below Balotra and loses itself southwestwards into the Rann of Kachchh.
