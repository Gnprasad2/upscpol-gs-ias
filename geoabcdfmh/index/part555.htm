20. Inadequacy of Extension Service


For the diffusion of agricultural innovations both in the irrigated and rain-fed areas, a team of skilled village level workers is required. There is much to be done in this area. Training of workers and their dedication can help the tradition bound farmers to modernise their agriculture.
