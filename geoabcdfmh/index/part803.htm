Fertiliser Units in India


At present, there are 70 fertiliser producing units in the country. The Fertiliser Corporation of India (FCI), the National Fertilser Limited, the Hindustan Fertiliser Corporation, the Rashtriya Chemicals and Fertiliser Limited (RCF), the Fertilisers and Chemicals Travancore Limited (FACT), the Madras Fertiliser Limited, the Paradwip Phosphate Limited (PPL), and the Projects and Development Limited India are some of the important public sector undertakings.
