6. Flood Plain Zoning


Flood plain zoning is another very effective method of flood management. It is based on information regarding land use in flood plains. Some areas are more prone to floods than others. Construction of buildings, factories, etc. in the zone adjacent to the river channels should be prohibited. 1 he areas occasionally flooded after a few years should be under green-belts in which social forestry is a priority.
