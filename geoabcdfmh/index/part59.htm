Physiographic Divisions of the Himalayas


For a systematic study of the physiography and relief, Himalayas may be divided into the following four divisions from north to south:


1. The Trans-Himalayas


2. The Greater Himalayas


3. The Lesser Himalayas


4. The Shiwaliks or the Outer Himalayas.
