1.5.1. Measures of Money Supply

There are several measures for the money supply, such as M1, M2, M3 and M4.

M1


 

M1 = Currency with public (coins, currency notes etc.) + net demand deposits held by the public with commercial banksM2 = M1 + Post office saving depositsM3 = M1 + Net time deposits of public with the banksM4 = M3 + Total Post office deposits (includes fixed deposits with the post offices but excludes National Savings Certificates)
