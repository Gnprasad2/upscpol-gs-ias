6. Technology Mission on Citrus

The mission was announced in 2006 for Vidarbha region.

♤ It was later extended to Marathwada and Chhindwara as well.

♤ The objective of the mission is:

o Production of disease free planting material of citrus

o Human Resource Development through training of horticulture officers on campus and training of citrus growers at their villages

o Demonstration of NRCC (National Research Centre for Citrus, Nagpur) technologies on citrus grower’s orchard for quality fruit production through scientific method of management

o Rejuvenation of declining citrus orchards
