IMPORTANCE

The importance of this policy lies in the fact that for the first time the entire energy sector is brought together in one policy and developing a long term vision. Energy is a key requirement and as investment is scaled up so do energy needs of an economy. Today even* sector needs energy be it agriculture, industrial activities or even service sector. The policy gives out energy needs, their sources and the way going forward. Its other importance lies in the fact it is bold in accepting realities and the challenges for the government in future.
