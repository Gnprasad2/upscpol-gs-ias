Genetic Blueprint


The concept of inheritance has been known for long- the fact that traits are passed down from generation to generation. These traits get passed on through "genes."

Genetic Blueprint is a map of a living organism's genes or genomes. Every organism is born with a unique genetic blueprint, that define many of the characteristics it has throughout.
