3. If the Supreme Court and the High Courts both were to be thought of as brothers in the administration of justice, the High Court has a larger jurisdiction but the Supreme Court still remains the elder brother. -Justice R.C Lahoti. In the context of the above statements describe the relationship between the apex court and the high courts in India.

Approach:

The Answer needs to highlight how both Supreme Court and High Courts have defined areas of jurisdiction under the provisions of constitution. Mention the provisions that make Supreme Court the apex court within India as well as the provisions that provide High Court a wider Jurisdiction. Keep in mind particularly Articles 132 to 136, 139A, 141, 144, 226 and 227. Also Remember Tirupati Balaji Developers Pvt. Ltd. and Ors. Vs. State of Bihar Case 2003.
