6. “The process of justice delivery in India has become a punishment in itself”. In reference to the above, examine the causes for a large number of under trials in the country. Do you think Supreme Court’s recent directive on Section 436A of CrPC would be able to address the issue?

Approach:

The answer should be divided into two parts, the first one addressing the causes for large number of undetrials in India. The second part should bring out why SC ruling is not enough for solving the problem. Reasons must be cited to justify the stand. Finally, end on the kind of reforms that are needed to truly solve the problem of large number of undertrials in India.
