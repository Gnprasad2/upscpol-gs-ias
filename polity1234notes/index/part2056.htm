3.4.1. Local Authorities’ Constituency

In any local authorities' constituency

(a) the electorate shall consist of members of such local authorities exercising jurisdiction in any place or area within the limits of that constituency as are specified in relation to that State in the Fourth Schedule of RPA, 1950

(b) every member of each such local authority within a local authorities' constituency shall be entitled to be registered in the electoral roll for that constituency
