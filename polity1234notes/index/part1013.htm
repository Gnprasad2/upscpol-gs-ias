5. There is a point of view that the office of Governor has outlived its relevance and needs to be abolished. Critically examine in the current context.

Approach:

♤ Introduce by mentioning the reason for constituting the office of governor.

♤ Mention the controversies and reasons for abolishing the office.

♤ Highlight the significance of the office.

♤ Conclude by mentioning some reform measures.
