Arguments against Judicial Activism:

♤ Judicial Activism is wrong, when Judges evolve policies, because they are not accountable to people.

♤ It can hurt the Federal Structure, when Judges do not adhere to Constitutional Principles while adjudicating complex issues e.g. "If State has a reservation policy, can the Centre override it?" "What is the implication if Centres tries to override the State on a subject listed in State List?"
