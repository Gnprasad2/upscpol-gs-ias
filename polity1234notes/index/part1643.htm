Shortcomings/Criticism:

⧫ Niti Aayog’s three-year action agenda has too wide approach for imminent challenges.

⧫ Limited focus on implementation challenges, bureaucratic reforms and government-citizen interaction, which is core to several good ideas remaining on paper.

⧫ It doesn’t focus adequately on the practical aspects of its recommendations. It lacks creation of a feedback loop, fixing accountability of bureaucrats and process reforms are missing.
