1.1. Corals

Corals are animals, even though they may exhibit some of the characteristics of plants and are often mistaken for rocks. Corals can exist as individual polyps (a small sea animal that has a body shaped like a tube), or in colonies and communities that contain hundreds to hundreds of thousands of polyps. Corals are found throughout the oceans, from deep, cold waters to shallow, tropical waters.
