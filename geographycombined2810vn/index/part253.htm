12. Economic significance of karst regions

⧫ Karst regions are often barren. The porosity of the rocks and the absence of surface drainage makes vegetation growth difficult. Therefore, these regions support short turf and poor grasses.

⧫ However, limestone vegetation in tropical regions is luxuriant because of all the year round rainfall.

⧫ Lead is the only mineral of importance in karst region. Lead occurs in veins in associations with limestone.

⧫ In addition to this, Limestone is used as building materials or quarried for the cement industry.
