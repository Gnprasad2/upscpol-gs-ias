1.4. Temperature

Like land masses, ocean water varies in temperature from place to place both at the surface and at great depths. Ocean water gets heated by solar energy just as land. The process of heating and cooling of the oceanic water is slower than land. This is due to higher specific heat of water as compared to land as a result of which greater amount of energy is required to raise the temperature of water as compared to land.
