India is part of Indian sub-continent and shares boundary with every country of this region. Land neighbours of India include Pakistan, Afghanistan, China, Nepal, Bhutan, Myanmar and Bangladesh. Most of our boundary with Pakistan and Bangladesh is almost man-made while boundary with other countries largely form a natural boundary. Sri Lanka and Maldives are the two island countries located in the Indian Ocean, which are our neighbours. Sri Lanka is separated from India by the Gulf of Mannar and Palk Strait.
