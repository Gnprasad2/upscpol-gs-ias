Negative Findings of the Report

 Non-Nutrition Component: The implementation and service delivery of the non- nutrition component needs to be further improved.

 Limited capacity of Anganwadi Workers: AWWs have received only limited sustained training and support across the states, due to which they have limited capacity.

 Limitations of Aganwadi Centers also acts as limitations of this scheme.

 Fund Adequacy: For non-nutrition components, the budgetary provisions have been studied to be insufficient.
