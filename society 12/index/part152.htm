3. Nature, Range and Patterns of Women’s Work

Women do various types of work. Their household work remains mostly invisible and unrecognised. Here it is essential to categorise various types of work done by women in terms of paid and unpaid work. This will give us a broad idea to understand the significance of women’s work both in the family and in the society.
