5.7. Way Forward

⧫ India’s flourishing relationship with Israel has not damaged its relations with other West Asian countries; on the contrary, the scale and scope of these relationships have been expanding

⧫ Stand alone visit of the Prime Minister to Israel in 2017 marked the de-hyphenation of relation with Israel from that with Palestinians and other Arab countries. This needs to be taken forward in a balanced way.
