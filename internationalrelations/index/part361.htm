2.8.1. Background

 Singapore was established as a trading post in 1819. It became a colony under British India, governed from Calcutta (1830-1867). It becam an independent state in 1965.

 Even though small, 700 Sq Kilometre area, Singapore is strategically located.

 It has become a melting pot of multiracial and multi cultural identities including people of Indian origi.

 

 Subhash Chandra Bose had resided in Singapore. Jawahar Lal Nehru visited in 1937 and 1946 2015 marked 50th anniversary of the bilateral relations and bilateral ties were elevated to a Strategic Partnership.Comprehensive Economic Cooperation Agreement (CECA) of 2005.
