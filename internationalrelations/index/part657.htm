5.2.2. Economic Ties

 Trade: UK is among India’s major trading partners and during the year 2016-17, UK ranked

16th in the list of India’s top 25 trading partners.

 As per trade statistics of MoC&I, India’s trade with UK in 2016-2017 was US $12.21 billion.

 Investment: UK is the 4 th largest inward investor in India, after Mauritius, Singapore and Japan with a cumulative equity investment of US $24.37 billion (April 2000- December 2016),
