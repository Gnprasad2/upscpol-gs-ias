3.4. Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty (CTBT)

 The CTBT is the Treaty banning all nuclear explosions - everywhere, by everyone.

 The Treaty was negotiated at the Conference on Disarmament in Geneva and adopted by the United Nations General Assembly. It opened for signature on 24 September 1996.

 Since then, the Treaty has reached near-universality. 183 countries have signed the Treaty.
