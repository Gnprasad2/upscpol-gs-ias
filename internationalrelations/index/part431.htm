1.2. Prime Ministers Visit to the Indian Ocean Countries 2015

India’s role as the "net security provider" in the Indian Ocean region received a major boost when Prime Minister Narendra Modi visited three India Ocean nations of Seychelles, Mauritius and Sri Lanka from March 10-14 2015.
