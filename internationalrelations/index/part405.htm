4.2.1. India and Pacific Island Countries: FIPIC

♤ Forum for India Pacific Cooperation (FIPIC) includes 14 of the island countries – Cook Islands, Fiji, Kiribati, Marshall Islands, Micronesia, Nauru, Niue, Palau, Papua New Guinea, Samoa, Solomon Islands, Tonga, Tuvalu, and Vanuatu. It was launched during the visit of Prime Minister Modi to Fiji in November 2014.
