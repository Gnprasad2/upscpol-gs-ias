4. India-Iran

4.1. Background

 India-Iran relations span millennia marked by meaningful interactions. The two countries shared a border till 1947 and share several common features in their language, culture and traditions.

 Independent India and Iran established diplomatic links on March 15, 1950. In addition to the Embassy in Tehran, India has two Consulates in Iran, one in Bandar Abbas and other in Zahedan.

 In recent times Iran has emerged as a key location for India’s connectivity initiatives for Afghanistan and Central Asia.
