2.1. What is ASEAN?

 The Association of South East Asian Nations (ASEAN) was founded in 1967 by Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines, Singapore, and Thailand to promote political and economic cooperation and regional stability. It based on the vision of an ASEAN Community comprised of three pillars, the Political-Security Community, Economic Community and Socio-Cultural Community.
