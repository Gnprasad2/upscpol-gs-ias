 SAARC Energy Centre (SEC), Islamabad

Established in 2006 with the mandate to act as a catalyst for the economic growth and development of South Asia region by initiating, coordinating and facilitating regional as well as joint and collective activities on energy. SEC would provide technical inputs for the SAARC Working Group (and other) meetings on Energy, and will facilitate accelerating the integration of energy strategies within the region by providing relevant information, state- of-the-art technology and expertise.
