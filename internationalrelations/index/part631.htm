 During the 2018 Visit of the President Emmanuel Macron the two countries issued Joint Strategic Vision of India-France Cooperation in the Indian Ocean region.

 In 2018, the two sides also signed agreement on reciprocal logistics support for armed forces; agreement will facilitate the reciprocal provision of logistic support, supplies and services between the armed forces of the two countries during authorised port visits, joint exercises and joint training among others.

 Two countries also signed an agreement on exchange and reciprocal protection of classified or protected information.
