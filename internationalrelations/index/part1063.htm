How do you become an OCI?

⧫ One can apply through the Indian embassy in your country of residence or within India at the local FRRO.

⧫ The entire process can take several months in some cases. Fees vary from nationality to nationality.

Sources:

⧫ Ministry of External Affairs

⧫ World Migration report 2018

⧫ Report of the High Level Committee on Indian Diaspora, http://indiandiaspora.nic.in/contents.htm

⧫ Ashok Rao, 6 February 2013, https://www.fairobserver.com/region/central_south_asia/indian-diaspora-past-present- and-future-part-iii/
