2.2. Economy

 Trade and economic relations have started to gather momentum again following the implementation of CEPA in 2010

 A revised trade target of USD 40 billion by 2015 was set by PM Singh and President Lee on 25 March 2012. However, bilateral trade has since declined to USD 18.13 billion in 2014-15, USD 16.56 billion in 2015-16, USD 16.82 billion in 2016-17, which finally recovered and posted a positive growth of 30% in the first seven months of 2017.
