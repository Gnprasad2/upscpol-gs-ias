“Enduring Global Partners in the 21st Century”.

 The June 2017 Joint statement called for Prosperity through Partnership.

 Today, the India-U.S. bilateral cooperation is broad-based and multi-sectoral, covering:

o trade and investment,

o defence and security,

o education, science and technology,

o cyber security, high-technology, civil nuclear energy, space technology and applications,

o clean energy, environment, agriculture and health.

o Vibrant people-to-people interaction

 These relations have acquired sustained support across the political spectrum in both countries
