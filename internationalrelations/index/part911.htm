2.2.5.1. International Seabed Authority

 An organization established by the UNCLOS, the International Seabed Authority, organizes and controls activities in the Area, particularly with a view to administering resources in the Area.

 It is based in Kingston, Jamaica,

 The Convention provides that activities in the Area shall be carried out for the benefit of mankind.

 Both the high-seas and the Area can only be used for peaceful purposes.
