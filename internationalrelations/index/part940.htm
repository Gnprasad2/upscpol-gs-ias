2.8. The International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA)

 

⧫ IRENA is an intergovernmental organisation that supports countries in their transition to a sustainable energy future, and serves as the principal platform for international cooperation, a centre of excellence, and a repository of policy, technology, resource and financial knowledge on renewable energy.⧫ The Agency is Headquartered in Abu Dhabi, UAE.
