4.1. Pacific Ocean and Island Countries

 The Pacific Ocean is the earth's largest ocean covering 46 per cent of water surface and 33 per cent of the earth's total surface, making it larger than the entire earth's land area.

 It is bounded by 41 sovereign states plus Taiwan, and 22 non-independent territories.

 

 It is rich in marine resources and accounts for 71 per cent of the world's ocean fishery catch.
