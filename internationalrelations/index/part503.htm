 In February 2014, India and Israel signed three important agreements on:

o Mutual Legal Assistance in Criminal Matters,

o Cooperation in Homeland and Public Security, and

o Protection of Classified Material.

 Under Cooperation in Homeland Security, four working groups in the areas of border management, internal security and public safety, police modernization and capacity building for combating crime, crime prevention and cyber crime were established.

 IPS officer trainees visited Israel in 2015 and 2016 for foreign exposure visit.

 There is ongoing cooperation on counter-terrorism issues, including through a Joint Working Group on Counter-Terrorism which held its last meeting in July, 2015.
