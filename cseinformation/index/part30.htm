2017

6. Highlight the importance of new objective that got added to the vision of Indian independence since the twenties of the last century.

2015

17. What were the major political, economic and social developments in the world which motivated the anti-colonial struggle in India?

20. “In many ways, Lord Dalhousie was the founder of modern India.” Elaborate.
