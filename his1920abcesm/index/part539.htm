● Nehru Report (1928)

First Indian effort to draft constitutional scheme. Recommended—

* dominion status

* not separate electorates, but joint electorates with reserved seats for minorities.

* linguistic provinces

* 19 fundamental rights

* responsible government at centre and in provinces.
