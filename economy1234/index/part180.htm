2.1. Government or Public Expenditure

Public expenditure refers to expenses incurred by public authorities – central government, state government and local authorities – for their own maintenance and also for the satisfaction of collective needs of the citizens and/or for promoting their economic and social welfare. For e.g. expenditure incurred by public authorities in running the government in the form of expenditure on administration and maintenance of law and order, as also on education, health, transportation, defence, social security etc.
