Figure: Number of broadband subscribers

Provision of Broadband in rural and remote areas helps in bridging the ‘digital divide’ and the widespread adoption of broadband in rural areas has a multiplier effect over the long-term. It can help improve productivity in rural areas, help overcome the constraints of an inadequate transport infrastructure and overall improve the quality of life in rural areas. Given the significant economic and social benefits, expanding affordable access to broadband has become a high priority for the Government. Various schemes have been launched for providing broadband connectivity to rural & remote areas.
