B. Argument against

During economic slowdown, the government has to incur deficit to boost the economy. When the aggregate demand is already low, adhering to the path of FC is counter-productive. For example, during 2008 crisis we have to abandon the targets under FRBM Act. To look into this issue further, NK Singh Committee has been set up by Finance Ministry.
