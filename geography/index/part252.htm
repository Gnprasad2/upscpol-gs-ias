CLIMATE OF INDIA



Weather is the momentary state of the atmosphere while climate refers to the average of the weather conditions over a longer period of time. India has hot monsoonal climate which is the prevalent climate in south and Southeast Asia. The monsoon regime emphasizes the unity of India with the rest of Southeast Asian region.
