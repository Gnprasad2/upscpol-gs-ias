Cereals

⧪ The cereals occupy more than half of the total cropped area in India.

⧪ The country produces about per cent cereals of the world and ranks third in production after China and U.S.A.

⧪ India produces a variety of cereals, which are classified as fi ne grains (rice, wheat), and coarse grains (jowar, bajra, maize, ragi), etc. Account of important cereals has been given in the following paragraphs:
