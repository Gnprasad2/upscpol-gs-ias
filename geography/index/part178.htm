Distribution of Temperate Cyclones

⧪ USA and Canada – extend over Sierra Nevada, Colorado, Eastern Canadian Rockies and the Great Lakes region, the belt extending from Iceland to Barents Sea and continuing over Russia and Siberia, winter storms over Baltic Sea, Mediterranean basin extending up to Russia and even up to India in winters (called western disturbances) and the Antarctic frontal zone.
