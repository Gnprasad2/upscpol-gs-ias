West flowing Rivers



Narmada

⧪ Origin – Amarkantak plateau (1,057m) (Shahdol district, Madhya Pradesh)

⧪ Total Length– 1,310 km (largest west fl owing river) Only 112 km navigable from mouth.

⧪ Flows 1,078 km in Madhya Pradesh Forms 32 km long boundary between M.P and Maharashtra.


⧪ Forms 40 km long boundary between Maharashtra & Gujarat Flows 160 km in Gujarat


⧪ Makes an estuary before entering into Gulf of Khambhat.


⧪ There are several islands in estuary formed by Narmada. Aliabet is an important estuary island.


⧪ States – M.P. Maharashtra, Gujarat

⧪ Landmarks – DhuanDhar falls also called cloud of mist (30m) located in Jabalpur district, Madhya Pradesh. This fall is located in a Gorge of marble.

⧪ Other falls- Mandhar falls (12m)Dardi fall (12m) Sahasradhara falls (8m)
