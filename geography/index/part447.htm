Air Transport

⧪ Air transport is the fastest mode of transport which has reduced distances and has led to drastic shrinking of the world. This mode of transport is indispensable when speed and time are the main considerations. One can easily cross and reach remote, inaccessible and hostile areas like lofty mountains, thick forests, marshy areas and sandy deserts by air transport which is almost

impossible by other modes of transport.

⧪ Air transport plays a vital role in times of emergency as well as in the event of natural and man- made calamities like floods, famines, epidemics and wars. Air transport is very essential for a vast

country like India where distances are so long and the terrain and climatic conditions so diverse.

⧪ The weather conditions in India are also quite congenial to air transport. Poor visibility due to clouds, fog and mist hinders air transport but India is lucky to have clear weather for most part of the year

except for a short duration in rainy season and foggy particularly in North India.
