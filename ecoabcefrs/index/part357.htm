Spread

The difference between the buying and selling prices of a share is called

spread. Higher the liquidity of a share lower its spread and vice versa. Also known as Jobber’s Turn or Margin or Hair cut.


Kerb Dealings

The transactions of stocks which take place outside the stock exchanges— unofficially and take place after the normal trading hours.
