DEBENTURE


An instrument of raising long-term loan by companies, having a maturity period bearing an interest (coupon rate). Theoretically they may be secured

or unsecured by assets such as land and buildings of the issuing company (known as collateral).

Debenture holders are provided with a prior claim on the earnings (by interest) and assets of the company in the situation of liquidation of the company over the preference and equity shareholders of the company.
